How to Use

Using Akemi hair grow spray is simple enough for a daily grooming routine. Follow these steps for the best experience.

01

Apply on a Dry or Towel-Dried Scalp

Part your hair where support is needed, such as the crown, hairline, or thinning-looking areas.

02

Spray at the Roots

Apply a few sprays directly to the scalp. Try not to waste the product on the outer hair shaft.

03

Massage for 30–60 Seconds

Massage gently with your fingertips. This helps spread the formula across the scalp and makes the routine feel more complete.

04

Let It Dry

Do not rinse it out. Let the spray dry, then style your hair as usual.

05

Stay Consistent

Use daily as directed. Hair appearance changes slowly, so a few weeks of consistent use usually gives a fairer impression than trying it once or twice.
