Shipping & Returns

At akemihairglowofficial.com©, we aim to ensure that every customer has a smooth and confident buying experience. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase, please review the return guidelines below.

Check Items on Delivery

We recommend inspecting your order carefully upon delivery to ensure:

  • The correct item has been received
  • Packaging is intact
  • The product meets your expectations

If there are any issues, contact support as soon as possible.

General Return & Refund Guidelines

Returns

  • You have 30 days from the date of delivery to request a return.
  • Items must be unused and in original condition.
  • Products must be returned in original packaging.
  • A valid proof of purchase or order confirmation is required.
  • All included materials (labels, inserts, packaging) must be intact.
  • Because this Site operates as a brand product platform, return eligibility may depend on the official supplier or checkout provider.

Important Note on Return Processing

AkemiHairGlowOfficial.com does not directly handle product fulfillment.

This means:

  • Returns are processed through the official product supplier or checkout partner
  • Return instructions are provided after purchase via email or order confirmation
  • Policies may vary slightly depending on the supplier handling your order

Refunds

  • Once your return request is approved, you will receive instructions from the official order support team
  • Returned items are inspected upon receipt
  • After approval, refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Please allow several business days for the refund to reflect, depending on your payment provider.

Shipping

  • Customers are typically responsible for return shipping costs, unless stated otherwise by the supplier
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable
  • If applicable, return shipping costs may be deducted from your refund

Exchanges

For product exchanges (replacement or alternative orders):

  • Please contact the official order support team provided at checkout
  • Exchange options may include: Replacement product, Store credit, or Refund (if applicable)
  • Availability depends on supplier-specific policies.

Exceptions to Returns

The following conditions may not qualify for returns:

  • Discounted or promotional items marked as final sale
  • Products that show visible signs of use
  • Items returned without original packaging or components
  • Requests made beyond the 30-day return window

How to Initiate a Return

To begin your return process:

  1. Locate your order confirmation email
  2. Contact the support team listed in your order details
  3. Request return instructions and authorization
  4. Follow the provided steps to ship the product back

If you cannot find your order details, you may contact us for guidance.
